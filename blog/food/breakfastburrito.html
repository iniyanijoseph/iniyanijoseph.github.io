Ingredients

  • 5 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup egg whites
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 lb diced potatoes
  • lb cheddar cheese
  • oz spinach
  • 2 diced bell peppers
  • 1 diced white onion

Directions

  • Combine the potatoes, peppers, spinach, and onion in a nonstick skillet. Cover and cook 5-6 minutes without stirring.
  • Separate egg yolks and whites.
  • Beat egg whites to stiff peaks.
  • Fold in egg yolks, cheese, and potato mixture in skillet and cook until fully cooked.
  • On a griddle, warm the tortillas and form burritos with the filling. Then seal burrito with some extra cheese.

Cost

Cost per burrito is about $2.50.
