Ingredients

  • 9 inch piecrust
  • 125 mL milk
  • 1 diced medium onion
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 beets
  • 3 eggs
  • salt

Directions

  • Boil beets until fork-tender
  • Sautee garlic and onions until carmelized
  • Puree beets, garlic, and onions
  • Mix in eggs and milk while pureeing
  • Pour into pie crust
  • Bake at for 45 minutes.
