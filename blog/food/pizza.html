Recently, I have had the opportunity to make more pizza. This recipe by Lopez-Alt is has been my favorite, and is quite easy [1].

Ingredients

Dough

  • 630g bread flour
  • 15g sugar
  • 10g kosher salt
  • 10g instant yeast
  • 32g oil
  • 420g lukewarm water

Toppings

  • 24 oz tomato sauce jar
  • 1 lb mozzarella cheese

Directions

  • Combine dry ingredients
  • Add wet ingredients and knead until the dough passes the windowpane test
  • Divide into 3 equal parts and cold ferment for 1-5 days.
  • Bake in a oven on the top rack. If possible, use a pizza stone or large cast iron pan.
