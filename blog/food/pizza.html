Recently, I have had the opportunity to make more pizza. This recipe by Lopez-Alt is has been my favorite, and is quite easy [1].

Ingredients

  • 630g bread flour
  • 15g sugar
  • 10g kosher salt
  • 10g instant yeast
  • 32g oil
  • 420g lukewarm water

Directions

  • Combine dry ingredients
  • Add wet ingredients and knead until the dough passes the windowpane test
  • Divide into 3 equal parts and cold ferment for 1-5 days.

Cost

  • Wheat Montana Bread Flour ($1.88)
  • Oil ($0.14)
  • Yeast ($0.22)
  • Salt ($0.04)
  • Sugar ($0.03)

This recipe yields 3 portions, for a cost of $0.77 per pizza. With toppings, this comes out to about $4 per pizza.
