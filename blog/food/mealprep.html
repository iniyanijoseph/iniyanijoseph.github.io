Weekly Meal Prep System

This system is designed around:

  •  750 calorie meals
  • high protein
  • high fiber
  • freezer-friendly prep
  • minimal weekday cooking
  • reusable components
  • minimal ingredient sprawl

Daily structure:

  • 1 lunch meal
  • 1 dinner meal
  • optional fruit and mozzarella sticks for additional hunger

Weekly Component Prep

Component 1 — Homemade Paneer

Makes approximately 16 portions.2

Ingredients
  • 3 gallons whole milk
  • 180g lemon juice or white vinegar
  • 10g salt
Method
  1. Heat milk to 90C.
  2. Stir occasionally to prevent scorching.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Slowly add lemon juice or vinegar while stirring gently.
  5. Rest 10 minutes.
  6. Strain through cheesecloth.
  7. Rinse lightly with cold water.
  8. Press under weight for 60–90 minutes.
  9. Refrigerate fully.
  10. Cube paneer.
Portioning
  • 16 containers of  135g paneer

Freeze most portions.

Component 2 — Challah

Ingredients
  • 180g warm water
  • 50g sugar
  • 7g active dry yeast
  • 480–540g all-purpose flour
  • 80g vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 6g salt

Optional:

  • sesame seeds
  • poppy seeds
Method
  1. Combine warm water, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Rest 5 minutes until foamy.
  3. Add flour, oil, eggs, egg yolk, and salt.
  4. Knead 8–10 minutes.
  5. Rise 1–2 hours.
  6. Divide into 6 strands.
  7. Braid loaf.
  8. Rise 30–60 minutes.
  9. Brush with egg white.
  10. Bake at 175C for 20–25 minutes.
Portioning
  • 12 slices of 75g

Freeze individually.

Component 3 — Roasted Vegetable Mix

Ingredients
  • 680g mushrooms
  • 475g onion
  • 475g bell pepper
  • 27g olive oil
  • 7g salt
  • 4g black pepper
  • 3g smoked paprika
  • 3g garlic powder
  • 2g cumin
Method
  1. Slice vegetables.
  2. Toss with olive oil and salt.
  3. Roast at 220C for 30–35 minutes.
  4. Stir halfway through.
  5. Cool completely.
Portioning
  • 12 containers of  82g3

Freeze.

Component 4 — Spinach Base

Ingredients
  • 1600g frozen spinach
  • 25g garlic
  • 15g olive oil
  • 8g salt
  • 5g cumin
  • 4g coriander
  • 3g turmeric
  • 2g fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
  • 2g chili flakes
Method
  1. Heat olive oil.
  2. Cook garlic 1 minute.
  3. Add spinach.
  4. Cook until excess moisture evaporates.
Portioning
  • 12 containers of 120g

Freeze.

Component 5 — Pinto Beans

Ingredients
  • 3 cans pinto beans (15.5 oz / 439g each)
  • 5g cumin
  • 4g chili powder
  • 3g smoked paprika
  • 2g garlic powder
  • salt to taste
Method
  1. Drain.
  2. Rinse thoroughly.
  3. Portion.
Portioning
  • 8 containers of 90g drained beans

Freeze.

Component 6 — Chickpeas

Ingredients
  • 3 cans chickpeas (15.5 oz / 439g each)
Method
  1. Drain.
  2. Rinse thoroughly.
  3. Portion.
Portioning
  • 8 containers of 90g drained chickpeas

Freeze.

Component 7 — Hummus

Ingredients
  • 1 can chickpeas (15.5 oz / 439g each)
  • 25g sesame seeds
  • 40g lemon juice
  • 15g olive oil
  • 10g garlic
  • 6g salt
  • 3g cumin
  • 60–120g cold water
Method
  1. Toast sesame seeds lightly.
  2. Add sesame seeds to blender.
  3. Blend until mostly smooth.
  4. Add chickpeas, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and olive oil.
  5. Blend while gradually adding cold water.
  6. Blend until smooth and creamy.
Portioning
  • 6 containers of 70–75g

Refrigerate or freeze.

Component 8 — Pizza Dough Balls

Ingredients
  • 630g bread flour4
  • 15g sugar
  • 10g kosher salt
  • 10g instant yeast
  • 32g olive oil
  • 420g lukewarm water
Method
  1. Combine dry ingredients.
  2. Add water and olive oil.
  3. Knead until smooth and elastic.
  4. Divide into 8 dough balls.
  5. Cold ferment 1–5 days.
Portioning
  • 8 dough balls of approximately 140g each

Freeze after at least 24 hours of fermentation if desired.

Meal Recipes

Meal 1 — Challah Lunch Plate

Components
  • 1 challah portion
  • 1 paneer portion
  • 1 roasted vegetable portion
  • 1 spinach portion
  • 1/2 hummus portion
Additional Ingredients
  • 20g balsamic vinegar
  • black pepper
  • flaky salt
Method
  1. Reheat roasted vegetables and spinach.
  2. Add balsamic vinegar.
  3. Warm challah.
  4. Plate with paneer and hummus.
Approximate Nutrition
  •  775 calories
  •  40g protein
  •  14g fiber

Meal 4 — Greek Salad Plate

Components
  • 1 paneer portion
  • 1 chickpea portion
  • 1/2 hummus portion
  • 1 challah portion
Additional Ingredients
  • 200g cucumber
  • 150g tomato
  • 20g lemon juice
  • 20g balsamic vinegar
  • oregano
  • salt
  • pepper
Method
  1. Chop cucumber and tomato.
  2. Combine with chickpeas.
  3. Add paneer.
  4. Dress with lemon juice, balsamic vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Serve with hummus and challah.
Approximate Nutrition
  •  785 calories
  •  42g protein
  •  17g fiber

Meal 5 — Personal Pizza

Components
  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1 roasted vegetable portion
  • 1/2 paneer portion
Additional Ingredients
  • 90g tomato sauce
  • 50g mozzarella
Method
  1. Stretch dough into a 23–25cm round.
  2. Add tomato sauce.
  3. Add mozzarella.
  4. Add paneer and roasted vegetables.
  5. Bake at maximum oven temperature.
Approximate Nutrition
  •  725 calories
  •  35g protein
  •  7g fiber
